Altrom is a leading manufacturer of high-quality solar products, serving customers with innovative & reliable solutions for renewable energy. With a focus on sustainability, performance, and affordability, Altrom is dedicated to making solar energy accessible to everyone.

At Altrom, we believe that solar energy has the power to transform the world, and we’re committed to making it easy and affordable for people to make the switch to clean energy. Our products are designed to be easy to install, low-maintenance, and long-lasting, so you can enjoy the benefits of solar energy for years to come.

Why Choose Altrom?

Founded in 1984, Altrom has established a reputation for excellence in the solar industry, thanks to its cutting-edge technology, experienced team, and commitment to customer satisfaction. Whether you’re looking for Solar Panels, Solar Water Heaters, or other solar-powered products, Altrom has a wide range of options to choose from.
